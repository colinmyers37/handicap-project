Nine Hole Calculation

To get your 9 whole handicap score just divide the course rating by two and put enter that as your course rating. This is common due to not always having the time to play a full 18 holes. If this is the case for you enter your score after nine holes but make sure to do the correct math when changing the course rating.

Averaging your Handicap

Your handicap is something that stays with you and should always be averaged. If you play 3 rounds and want to calculate your handicap over those 3 rounds you can take the handicap for each round and at those numbers together. After you get the sum of the numbers added together, divide them by the number of rounds you played and that is your average handicap
